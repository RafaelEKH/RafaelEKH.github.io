About the chess

The chessgame on this site is completly written by me. Therefore its performance is poor and the functions are very limited. It is functioning as a real life board. The only programmed limitation in the moves, that you can't take your own pieces. Instead you select another piece. The selected piece is highlighed by the yellow square. Currently, if you want to start a new game, you have to refresh the site.

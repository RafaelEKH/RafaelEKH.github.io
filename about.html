About the chess

The chessgame on this site is completly written by me. Therefore its performance is poor and the functions are very limited. It is functioning as a real life board. Turns and basic legal moves are implemented. To select a piece you have to click on it. The selected piece is highlighed by the yellow square. Only pieces of the active side can be selected. To remove the selection, click the selected or any illegal move. To select any other piece, you don't have to remove the current selection, just click on the other piece you want to select. Currently, if you want to start a new game, you have to refresh the site.
